Free financial tools

Know your numbers. Own your future.

Simple, powerful tools to help you budget, save, invest, and get out of debt — no spreadsheet degree required.

📊
Budget planner
See exactly where your money goes every month. Enter your income and expenses and instantly know what's left to save or invest.
🎯
Savings tracker
Set a savings goal, a deadline, and how much you can set aside each month — and watch the math show you what's possible.
📈
Wealth calculator
Enter an amount, an interest rate, and a time horizon to see what your money could become if you invest it — the math is powerful.
💳
Debt payoff planner
List your debts and pick a payoff strategy — avalanche or snowball — to get a clear, actionable plan to become debt free.
🧮
Net worth tracker
Add up your assets and subtract your debts to see your true financial picture. Knowing your number is step one to growing it.
🏦
Emergency fund calculator
Find out exactly how much you need in your emergency fund — and how long it will take to build it based on what you can save monthly.
